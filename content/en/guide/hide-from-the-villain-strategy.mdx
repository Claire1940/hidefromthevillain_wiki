Understanding the layout of each map and identifying optimal hiding spots is crucial for effective **hide from the villain strategy**. The best hiding spots offer multiple escape routes, allowing you to reposition if the villain discovers your location. Avoid predictable corners or dead-ends.

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Clustering">

</Callout>

### Optimal Hiding Spot Characteristics

| Characteristic | Description | Strategic Value | Example |
| :------------- | :---------- | :-------------- | :------ |
| **Multiple Exits** | Allows for quick escapes if a spot is compromised. | Prevents being cornered; maintains mobility. | Rooms with two or more doors/windows. |
| **Line-of-Sight Breaks** | Areas with objects or structures to break the villain's vision. | Makes tracking difficult; enables juking. | Tall crates, large furniture, tight corridors. |
| **Proximity to TEMP V** | Hiding near TEMP V spawns (when safe) for quick power-ups. | Grants access to counter-play abilities when needed. | Behind a wall near a known TEMP V spawn. |
| **Unpredictability** | Spots that are less commonly checked by villains. | Increases duration of undetected hiding. | Less obvious corners, elevated platforms. |
