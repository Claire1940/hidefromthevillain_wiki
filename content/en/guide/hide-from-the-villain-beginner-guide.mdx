<Section icon={Shield} title="Map Awareness & Positioning Strategy">

Winning as a survivor depends heavily on your understanding of the environment. Every map has "Dead Zones"—areas with only one entrance—that you should avoid at all costs. Instead, look for "Looping Zones," where you can run the Villain around obstacles if you are detected.

**Key Positioning Rules:**
- **Exit Awareness**: Never enter a room without knowing where the second exit is.
- **Noise Control**: Crouching reduces your sound profile, making it harder for the Villain to track you through walls.
- **Rotation**: Once the Villain searches your area and leaves, rotate to a new position. They are likely to double-back if they suspect someone is nearby.

<Callout type="tip" title="Environmental Cues">
Listen for the Villain's unique sound effects. Each skin often has specific audio cues or music that gets louder as they approach. Learning these sounds allows you to react before they even appear on your screen.
</Callout>
