<Section icon={Target} title="Mastering Stealth and Hiding Spots">
Effective stealth is your best defense in Hide From The Villain. Knowing where and how to hide can significantly extend your survival time. This isn't just about finding a corner; it's about anticipating the villain's moves and having an exit strategy.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Positioning">
    Upon spawning, immediately move to an area with multiple exits. Avoid central, open spaces. Your first few seconds are critical for setting up a strong defensive position.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Utilize Cover and Line of Sight">
    Always stay behind obstacles that break the villain's line of sight. Corners, large furniture, and dark areas are your best friends. Avoid moving when the villain is in direct view.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Listen for Sound Cues">
    Pay attention to the villain's footsteps or ability sounds. These audio cues are invaluable for predicting their approach and planning your next move. Sound is often a better indicator than visual range.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Dynamic Hiding">
    Don't stay in one spot for too long, especially after being spotted or if the villain lingers nearby. Reposition to a new hiding spot once the immediate threat has passed to avoid being found on a second sweep.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Escape Route Awareness">
    Before committing to any hiding spot, always know your potential escape routes. Being trapped in a perfect hiding spot with no way out is a common mistake.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Common Hiding Mistakes">
- **Hiding in dead ends**: Leads to instant capture once discovered.
- **Clustering with other survivors**: Makes it easier for the villain to find multiple targets.
- **Staying put after being seen**: Villains often double back to check previously spotted areas.
- **Ignoring sound cues**: Missing vital information about the villain's proximity.
</Callout>
