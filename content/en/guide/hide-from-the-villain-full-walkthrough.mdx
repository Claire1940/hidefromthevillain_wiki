<Section icon={Shield} title="Survivor's Full Walkthrough: Hiding and Evasion">
As a survivor, your journey begins with immediate evasion. The villain will be actively hunting from the start, so knowing the map and effective hiding spots is crucial.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Spawn & Movement">
    Upon spawning, immediately assess your surroundings. Look for nearby cover and plan a route to a less exposed area. Avoid open spaces. Move with purpose, but avoid making obvious, loud movements that could alert the villain.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Finding Effective Hiding Spots">
    The best hiding spots offer multiple escape routes and good line of sight to monitor the villain's movements. Corners, behind large objects, and elevated positions can be effective.
    <Callout type="info">
    **Example Hiding Spots:**
    - Behind crates in storage areas.
    - Inside small, enclosed rooms with only one entrance.
    - On rooftops or high ledges if the map allows.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Using Stealth and Awareness">
    Continuously listen for the villain's footsteps or abilities. Use camera controls to look around corners before committing to a path. If the villain is nearby, remain still and break line of sight.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Resource Management (First Aid Kits)">
    If you take damage, use First Aid Kits to recover. These are vital for extending your survival time. Prioritize finding and saving them for critical moments.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Surviving Until Rescue">
    The ultimate goal is to survive until the rescue timer runs out. Focus on staying alive, not on confronting the villain unless absolutely necessary.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Clumping Together">
Never hide with too many other survivors in one spot. A single villain sweep can expose the entire group, leading to quick eliminations. Spread out and increase the villain's search area.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="TEMP V Powers: Turning the Tables">
TEMP V is a game-changer for survivors, granting temporary powers to fight back. Knowing when and how to use these powers is key to your survival and potential counter-play.

| TEMP V Power Type | Effect                                        | Best Use Case                                      | Risk Factor |
| :---------------- | :-------------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------- | :---------- |
| **Speed Boost**   | Greatly increases movement speed.             | Escaping a direct chase, reaching a safe zone.     | Low         |
| **Invisibility**  | Renders you invisible for a short duration.   | Sneaking past a patrolling villain, repositioning. | Medium      |
| **Stun Blast**    | Stuns the villain in a small radius.          | Creating distance during a chase, protecting allies. | High        |
| **Force Field**   | Creates a temporary shield against attacks.   | Buying time for escape or waiting for rescue.      | Medium      |

<Callout type="success" title="Strategic TEMP V Usage">
The most effective use of TEMP V is not always offensive. Often, it's best used defensively to create distance, escape pressure, or protect a crucial moment, rather than directly engaging the villain.
</Callout>

**How to Acquire and Use TEMP V:**
TEMP V can be found randomly on maps during a round. Additionally, some codes (like **GAMEISBACK** and **SORRY4SHUTDOWN**) provide TEMP V as a reward, giving you a head start.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate TEMP V Items">
    Keep an eye out for glowing TEMP V pickups scattered across the map. These are often in less obvious, but still accessible, locations.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Assess the Situation">
    Before activating, consider your current proximity to the villain, your escape routes, and the overall round timer. Don't waste it in a safe spot.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Activate and Utilize">
    Once activated, use the power decisively. If it's a Speed Boost, commit to an escape. If it's a Stun Blast, time it perfectly to maximize its effect.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Target} title="Advanced Tips for Both Roles">
Mastering Hide From The Villain requires understanding the nuances of both roles and adapting your strategy.

**For Survivors:**
- **Map Awareness:** Learn the layout of each map, including common hiding spots, TEMP V spawn locations, and escape routes.
- **Villain Prediction:** Try to anticipate the villain's patrol patterns. Are they methodical or are they focusing on specific areas?
- **Communication (if applicable):** If playing with friends, coordinate movements and call out villain locations.
- **Decoy Tactics:** Sometimes, making a small amount of noise in one direction can draw the villain away from your actual hiding spot.

**For Villains:**
- **Psychological Warfare:** Use your presence to create fear and force survivors into mistakes.
- **Corner Trapping:** Guide survivors into dead ends or areas with limited escape options.
- **Ability Timing:** If your villain has special abilities, learn their cooldowns and optimal usage times.
- **Resource Denial:** If you see a survivor heading for a TEMP V pickup, try to cut them off.

| Role      | Key Advanced Skill             | Description                                                               |
| :-------- | :----------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Survivor** | **Route Optimization**         | Planning multi-stage escape routes that utilize cover and obscure vision. |
| **Villain**  | **Predictive Tracking**        | Anticipating where survivors will go based on their last known location.  |
| **Both**  | **Adaptability**               | Adjusting strategy based on the opponent's playstyle and current game state. |

<Callout type="warning" title="Don't Be Predictable">
Whether you're hiding or hunting, avoid predictable patterns. Survivors who always hide in the same spot will be found, and villains who always patrol the same route will be outsmarted. Mix up your approach!
