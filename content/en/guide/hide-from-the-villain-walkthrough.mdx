<Section icon={Shield} title="Mastering Hiding Spots and Evasion Techniques">
Effective hiding is the cornerstone of survival in **hide from the villain**. It's not just about finding a dark corner, but understanding the villain's patrol patterns and having an escape plan.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Prioritize Multi-Exit Locations">
    Always seek out rooms or areas that offer at least two distinct escape routes. This prevents you from being cornered and gives you options during a chase.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Use Line of Sight to Your Advantage">
    Break the villain's line of sight by using obstacles, furniture, and corners. If they can't see you, they can't chase you effectively. Crouch whenever possible to reduce your visibility.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Avoid Obvious Hiding Spots">
    Common spots like under beds or inside lockers are often the first places a seasoned villain will check. Opt for less conventional cover, such as behind large objects or in elevated positions if the map allows.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Listen for Sound Cues">
    Pay close attention to in-game sound effects. The villain's footsteps or ability activations can give you crucial warnings about their proximity, allowing you to reposition before they spot you.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Don't Stack with Other Survivors">
    While it might feel safer, clustering with too many survivors makes you all vulnerable. One villain sweep can expose an entire group. Spread out to increase overall team survival chances.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Hiding Spot Caution">
Never rely on a single hiding spot for the entire round. Villains often re-check areas, especially if they suspect a survivor was there. Be ready to rotate to a new location once they leave.
</Callout>

### Common Hiding Spot Types

| Hiding Type       | Description                                  | Best Use Case                               | Risk Level |
| :---------------- | :------------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------ | :--------- |
| **Cover**         | Behind large objects (crates, walls, pillars) | Quick repositioning, breaking line of sight | Low        |
| **Concealment**   | Dark corners, shadows, dense foliage        | Static hiding, waiting out patrols          | Medium     |
| **High Ground**   | Elevated platforms, ledges (if accessible)  | Vision over villain, unexpected angles      | Medium     |
| **Obscured Path** | Areas with complex pathways, multiple doors   | Evasion during chase, confusing villain     | Low        |

</Section>
