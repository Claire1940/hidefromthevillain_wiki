<Section icon={Shield} title="Survivor Strategies: Hiding and Stealth">
As a survivor, your primary objective in **Hide From The Villain** is to evade the villain until rescue arrives. Stealth and intelligent movement are your best allies. Knowing when to hide and when to move is critical for prolonged survival.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Prioritize Exit Routes">
    Always position yourself near multiple exits. A hiding spot without an escape path is a death trap once the villain discovers you. Plan your next move before the villain even sees you.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Utilize Line of Sight">
    Break the villain's line of sight by using corners, obstacles, and environmental features. Avoid open areas, especially when the villain is nearby, as these offer no cover.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Avoid Grouping Up">
    Resist the urge to cluster with other survivors. A single villain sweep can eliminate multiple players if everyone is hiding in the same predictable location. Spread out and increase the villain's search area.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Reposition After Checks">
    If the villain searches your area and moves on, don't stay frozen. Slowly and cautiously reposition to a new hiding spot. Villains often double back to re-check successful search zones.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
