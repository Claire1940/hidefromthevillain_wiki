As a survivor in Hide From The Villain, your primary goal is to stay hidden and alive until rescue arrives. This requires a deep understanding of map layouts, effective hiding spots, and strategic use of TEMP V powers when the situation demands a fight back.

### Optimal Hiding Strategies

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Exit Awareness">
    Always choose hiding spots that offer **multiple escape routes**. Dead-end corners are traps waiting to happen if the villain finds you.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Stealth Over Speed">
    When the villain is nearby, prioritize **stealthy movement** and breaking line of sight. Rushing into the open often leads to capture.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Avoid Grouping">
    Do not cluster with too many other survivors. A single villain sweep can expose multiple players in predictable hiding spots.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Rotate Positions">
    After the villain searches your area and leaves, **reposition yourself**. Villains often re-check successful search zones.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

| **Timing**        | Best used when facing high pressure or to create space.      | Avoid wasting it during calm periods or in unavoidable situations. |
