<Section icon={Shield} title="Survivor Strategy and Stealth Mechanics">

Survival in Hide From the Villain Roblox is not just about staying still; it is about active stealth and predictive movement. The villain has superior speed and detection capabilities, meaning survivors must use the environment to break line of sight and create distance. Relying on a single hiding spot for the entire round is a recipe for disaster, as experienced villains often re-check high-traffic zones.

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="Exit Awareness">
    Always choose hiding spots with at least two exit routes. Being cornered in a dead-end room is the most common cause of elimination.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Position Rotation">
    Once the villain checks your area and moves on, rotate to a new quadrant. Staying in the same spot makes you predictable.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Line of Sight">
    Use corners, furniture, and verticality to stay out of the villain's vision. Crouch to reduce your profile and muffle movement noise.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

Effective survivors prioritize the "Help Arrives" timer. Your goal is to bleed the clock. If you are spotted, your priority shifts from hiding to "kiting"—leading the villain through complex routes to waste their time while your teammates stay hidden.

<Callout type="success" title="Pro Survival Tip">
Don't cluster with other survivors. If the villain finds a group, they can often secure multiple eliminations in a single sweep. Spread out to force the villain to travel further between targets.
</Callout>

</Section>
