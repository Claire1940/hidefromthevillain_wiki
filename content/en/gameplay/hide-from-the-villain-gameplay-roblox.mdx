<Section icon={Target} title="Survivor Strategies: Hiding and Evasion">
As a survivor, your primary goal is to remain undetected and outlast the villain. Effective hiding and evasion are crucial for success in Hide From The Villain gameplay. This involves not just finding a good spot, but also understanding the villain's movement patterns and utilizing the map to your advantage.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Movement and Cover">
    Upon spawning, immediately move towards areas with ample cover. Avoid open spaces and corridors where you can be easily spotted. Look for rooms or sections of the map with multiple exits to prevent being cornered.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Utilizing Stealth">
    Walk or crouch whenever possible to minimize your noise footprint. Running should be reserved for escaping direct chases. Pay attention to sound cues – the villain's footsteps or abilities can alert you to their proximity.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Dynamic Hiding Spots">
    Don't stay in one hiding spot for too long, especially if the villain has recently searched the area. Villains often re-check previous locations. Rotate between different safe zones as the round progresses.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Breaking Line of Sight">
    If spotted, focus on breaking the villain's line of sight using obstacles, corners, and environmental elements. This can buy you precious time to find a new hiding spot or activate a power.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Predictable Hiding">
Do not hide in obvious spots that are easily checked by the villain. Common areas like behind a single box or in a corner of a small room are often the first places they look. Think creatively and use verticality when available.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Map} title="Mastering Maps and Hiding Spots">
Effective Hide From The Villain gameplay heavily relies on knowing the maps inside and out. Understanding layouts, common villain patrol routes, and optimal hiding spots can drastically increase your survival rate. While specific map names aren't always provided, the principles of good positioning remain consistent.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="High-Value Hiding Spots">
    - **Multi-exit rooms**: Areas with two or more exits offer escape routes.
    - **Verticality**: Use elevated platforms, vents, or ledges.
    - **Dynamic cover**: Objects that can be interacted with or moved.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Risky Zones to Avoid">
    - **Dead ends**: Corners or small rooms with only one entry/exit point.
    - **Open corridors**: Easy for villains to spot and chase.
    - **Predictable locations**: Behind the same set of boxes every round.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

**Map Awareness Tips**

| Strategy             | Description                                                                 | Benefit                                                   |
| :------------------- | :-------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :-------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Route Planning**   | Mentally map out escape routes from your current position.                  | Reduces panic, increases survival chances during chases.   |
| **Villain Pathing**  | Observe where the villain typically patrols and where they spend most time. | Helps predict their movements and avoid those areas.       |
| **Noise Management** | Be aware of objects that make noise when interacted with.                     | Prevents accidental detection by the villain.             |
| **Team Spacing**     | Avoid clumping with other survivors in one spot.                             | Prevents the villain from catching multiple players at once. |
