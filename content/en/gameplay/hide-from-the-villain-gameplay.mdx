<Section icon={Shield} title="Survival Strategies for New Players">

For those just starting their journey in **hide from the villain gameplay**, the first few rounds can feel overwhelming. The villain moves significantly faster than survivors, and many hiding spots that look safe are actually "death traps" with only one exit. To increase your survival rate, you must adopt a "movement-first" stealth mindset. This means you should always be positioned near a secondary exit or a loop-able structure.

New players often make the mistake of sprinting everywhere. Sprinting leaves trails and makes noise, making it easy for the villain to track you down. Instead, use the environment to muffle your presence. Crouch-walking and staying behind large obstacles while the villain is nearby is far more effective than a desperate dash across an open field.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Spawn & Scouting">
    As soon as the round starts, move away from the spawn point. Identify the nearest TEMP V spawn locations and locate at least two distinct hiding zones.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Establish Stealth Priority">
    Avoid standing in open areas. Use corners and verticality to stay out of the villain's primary field of view. Do not cluster with other survivors, as this attracts unnecessary attention.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Monitor Villain Pressure">
    Listen for audio cues. The closer the villain is, the more you should prioritize breaking line of sight. If the villain searches your room and leaves, rotate to a new position immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Secure Rescue Window">
    When the rescue timer is nearly finished, move toward the extraction point cautiously. Do not reveal yourself too early, as villains often camp extraction zones in the final seconds.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Survival Tip">
A hiding place with no escape route is a coffin. Always ensure you have a "Plan B" route if the villain enters your immediate vicinity. If you are cornered, that is the only time you should consider using your TEMP V powers defensively.
</Callout>

The defining feature of **hide from the villain gameplay** is the TEMP V system. Inspired by popular superhero media, TEMP V is a consumable item that grants hiders temporary abilities to combat the villain. This turns a standard survival game into a tactical action experience. When a survivor consumes TEMP V, they gain random powers—ranging from super strength to flight—which can be used to stun or displace the villain.

Using TEMP V is a double-edged sword. While it gives you the power to fight back, it also reveals your location to the villain. It should be used as a "clutch" tool rather than a primary playstyle. If the villain is about to catch a teammate or if you are trapped in a dead-end, activating TEMP V can provide the window needed to escape and reset the stealth loop.
