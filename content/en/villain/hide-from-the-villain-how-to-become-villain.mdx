Victory in **Hide From The Villain** is often determined by your positioning. Whether you are the hunter or the hunted, understanding the map layout is paramount. While specific map names may change with updates, the behavioral strategies remain the same.

**Key Map Strategies:**
- **Exit Awareness**: Never hide in a room with only one door. If the villain enters, you are trapped. Always favor areas with windows, multiple doors, or verticality.
- **Rotation**: Once a villain searches an area and leaves, that spot is temporarily "safe," but they will likely return. Move to a new angle or a different floor to stay unpredictable.
- **Noise Management**: Sprinting creates noise and visual cues. Use the walk or crouch functions when the villain is nearby to avoid detection.
