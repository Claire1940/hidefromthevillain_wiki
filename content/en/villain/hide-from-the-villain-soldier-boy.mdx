Winning as a survivor in **hide from the villain soldier boy** requires more than just sitting in a corner. Skilled villains will check common hiding spots, so your positioning must be dynamic. The best survivors are those who understand the "flow" of the map and know exactly where the villain is at all times.

**Map Play Comparison:**

| Tactic | Pros | Cons |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Edge Camping** | Low visibility, hard for villain to stumble upon | No escape if discovered, far from rescue |
| **Central Rotation** | Easy access to all exits, many TEMP V spawns | High risk of being spotted, constant pressure |
| **Group Hiding** | Can distract the villain for each other | One sweep can reveal the entire group |
| **Stealth Crawling** | Reduces noise, harder to see over low cover | Extremely slow, vulnerable in open areas |

- **Noise Control**: Avoid jumping or running unnecessarily. The villain can hear footsteps, and loud movements give away your position through walls.
- **Line of Sight**: Use the third-person camera to look around corners without exposing your character model.
- **Rescue Timing**: When the rescue arrives, don't sprint immediately. Wait for the villain to commit to a chase elsewhere, then make your move.
